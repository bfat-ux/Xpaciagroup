About XPACIA Group

A multidisciplinary firm delivering thoughtful architecture across Nigeria and Africa.

XPACIA Group is a design-led, client-centric architecture, engineering, construction, and real estate firm operating across Nigeria and the African region.

Founded on the belief that great buildings begin with understanding people, XPACIA delivers tailored solutions that respond to each client's unique needs, scale, and objectives. Our approach is flexible — supporting clients at any stage of their project journey, from early design through full delivery.

At the core of our work is architecture that is thoughtful, functional, and context-aware. This design foundation is supported by strong technical expertise in engineering, construction, and project management, allowing us to offer integrated services without compromising quality or clarity.

XPACIA's multidisciplinary structure ensures seamless coordination across all project phases, reducing risk, improving efficiency, and delivering outcomes that meet both aesthetic and practical goals.

We work across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors, partnering with private clients, corporate organizations, and public institutions to deliver projects that are well-designed, well-built, and built to last.
